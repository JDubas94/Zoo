About the zoo

  • We have been working since 2022 - during this time, not a single animal has been harmed. Moreover, some of the animals have had offspring.
  • We work legally - we have all the necessary permits.
  • More than 40 species of animals and birds - the geography of pets from Africa to Australia.
  • All our animals are not aggressive and undergo regular veterinary examinations - the animals are adapted to human interaction, allow themselves to be petted, and are also healthy and well-groomed
  • There is a photographer on site - every weekend free of charge. All photos are available on our Facebook page

Zoo infrastructure

Restaurants

On the territory of the zoo there are restaurants, a summer cafe and vending machines selling drinks

Souvenirs

In the zoo you will find several souvenir shops that are open from 10 am

Children's Zoo

In the southern part there is a children's zoo, with direct contact with some animals

Cable car

For convenience, there is a cable car on the territory of the zoo, from where you can enjoy a beautiful view from above
